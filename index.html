Welcome to Slug Security

Welcome to Slug Security, UC Santa Cruz's student-run cybersecurity club! If you've ever been curious about hacking, security, or just want to learn more about keeping others or yourself safe online, you've come to the right place. Our club is dedicated to fostering a fun, inclusive community where members can learn, teach, and collaborate on cybersecurity projects. Whether you're an experienced hacker or have never touched a line of code before, we welcome students of all backgrounds and skill levels to join us in exploring the world of cybersecurity. We offer a variety of events throughout the year, including workshops, talks, CTF competitions, and more.

Meeting Times and Locations

We try to hold a workshop every week, Monday 2-4 PM at E2-506. All workshop resources and practice challenges will be available online. Any updates regarding meeting times and locations will be posted on our Discord server and other social media platforms which can be found at the bottom of the page.
