Welcome to Slug Security

Welcome to Slug Security, the cybersecurity club run by UC Santa Cruz students! If you're interested in learning more about hacking, cybersecurity, or how to stay safe online, you've come to the right place. Our club is all about creating an inclusive and enjoyable environment where members can learn, teach, and work together on cybersecurity projects. Whether you're a seasoned hacker or you're completely new to coding, we invite students of all backgrounds and skill levels to join us in exploring the exciting world of cybersecurity. We offer a range of events throughout the year, such as workshops, talks, CTF competitions, and more.

Meeting Times and Locations

We try to hold a workshop every week, Monday 2-4 PM at E2-506. All workshop resources and practice challenges will be available online. Any updates regarding meeting times and locations will be posted on our Discord server and other social media platforms which can be found at the bottom of the page.
